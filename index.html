🌾 About HamiKisan

HamiKisan (हामी किसान - "Together Farmers") is a lightweight agricultural platform designed specifically for Nepalese farmers. The app combines modern technology with traditional farming knowledge to help farmers improve crop yields, access market information, and connect with agricultural experts.
